SCORE balanced energy policy

The SCORE minimum energy policy balances energy usage between locomotive types, to reduce energy cost while minimizing travel time. This policy uses maximum regenerative braking. The policy uses an LP optimization algroithm to solve the optimal levels of diesel, battery, and fuel cell along the route.


Custom Power and Braking Policy - Maximum Braking

All available locomotive braking is applied (dynamic and regenerative) when necessary to minimize travel time resulting in fastest transit time.


Custom Power and Braking Policy - Maximum Regenerative

The analysis maximizes the usage of regenerative braking for energy storage.


Custom Power and Braking Policy - Coasting

The analysis using coasting to minimize the use of any braking, which results in a slower transit time.


Order of Locomotive Energy Usage

The order of the consist locomotives types which are used first to meet the power demands along a route.
